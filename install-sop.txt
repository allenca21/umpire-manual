UMPIRE'S MANUAL — Get it on your phone
========================================

A 3-minute setup. Works offline at the field once saved.

OPEN THIS LINK IN YOUR BROWSER:
https://YOUR-URL-HERE.netlify.app    ← replace with the real URL


iPhone (Safari)
---------------
1. Open Safari and go to the link above.
2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
   (square with an up-arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
5. Tap the new Umpire Manual icon on your home screen.
6. On the prompt, tap "Download (~17 MB)" so it works offline.
   Wait a few seconds for it to finish.


Android (Chrome)
----------------
1. Open Chrome and go to the link above.
2. You may see an "Install" banner — tap it.
   If not, tap the menu (3 dots, top-right).
3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Install" to confirm.
5. Tap the new Umpire Manual icon on your home screen.
6. On the prompt, tap "Download (~17 MB)" so it works offline.
   Wait a few seconds for it to finish.


Once installed:
- Tap the icon any time — no Wi-Fi needed after Step 6.
- Use the menu (three lines, top-left) for Table of Contents,
  Search, and Bookmarks.
- Tap the "A" in the header to make the text bigger.
- Tap the bookmark icon next to the page number to save a page.
- Swipe left/right to flip pages; tap the page number to jump.

Questions? Contact your crew chief.
